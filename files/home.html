About Us

Welcome to the Meal Management System — a modern web application designed to simplify and streamline the entire process of managing daily meals, expenses, and groceries in a mess or shared living environment.

Built with passion by a team of five dedicated Computer Science students from Daffodil International University, our mission is to provide a hassle-free experience for mess members by offering automated calculations, intuitive interfaces, and real-time data handling.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Meal Management System?

The Meal Management System is a web-based platform designed for university hall students to view, book, and manage their daily meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) with ease and transparency.

How can I register on the platform?

Click on the “Sign Up” button in the navigation bar and fill in your student details such as name, email, contact number, and password to create an account.

How do I know what meals are available?

Visit the “Menu” section from the navigation bar. You can browse meals categorized under Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner, each showing pictures, prices, and descriptions.

How can I contact support for queries or issues?

Scroll to the “Contact Us” section at the bottom of the page. Fill out the form with your query, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
